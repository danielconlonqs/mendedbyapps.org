About us

Apps with purpose — built for the people who actually need them

Mended by Apps was founded on a simple idea — that the best apps are the ones that fix something real. Not feature-rich distractions. Not solutions looking for a problem. Just tools that make a genuine difference to the people using them.

We build for people who are too busy to figure out complicated software — people who need something that just works, the moment they pick up their phone. Every product we make is offline-first, privacy-first, and designed around the actual person using it.

Our architecture is built around a core principle: your data belongs to you. Everything you enter into our apps stays on your device. We have no servers storing your information, no database that could be breached, and nothing to sell to advertisers. We make money from subscriptions — not from you as a data product.

Our roadmap is driven by the people who need our apps. If you have a problem that deserves an app, we want to hear about it.
